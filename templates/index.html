FAQ' s

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
Q. What is dynamic emotion recognition? Dynamic emotion recognition is the process of identifying and understanding human emotions in real-time, based on facial expressions and speech patterns.
Q. How does face emotion recognition work? Face emotion recognition uses computer vision techniques to analyze facial expressions and identify emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, etc.
Q. How does speech emotion recognition work? Speech emotion recognition analyzes speech signals to detect and classify emotions conveyed through voice characteristics, such as pitch, intensity, and tempo.
Q. Who are the team members of this project? Our team consists of experienced students in the fields of AI, computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning.
Q. Can I use the face and speech emotion recognition simultaneously? No, you can perform face emotion recognition and speech emotion recognition independently right now.
